About Us

Our Leadership

Mohammed Enayetur Rahman


President/CEO

Rahman has more than 31 years of experience in the semiconductor industry in engineering management, strategy making, resource building, product planning and risk management. He started his career as a Design Engineer at AMD and held senior technical and management positions with Oki Semiconductor, Virtual Silicon and Syntera Communication. During his 12+ years of tenure in AMD, he was a pioneer in designing several interface logic chips. He has patents on data communication systems. Rahman received his B.Sc. in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology and MS in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Oregon State University, Corvallis, OR. He also attended engineering management courses in Stanford University.

Mizan Rahman Ph.D.


VP Engineering


Dr. Mizan Rahman joined Ulkasemi with more than 30 years of technical and executive management experience in Silicon Valley high tech industry. He has expertise in hardware design, verification, manufacturing and customer support. Mizan has extensive hands on design expertise on ASIC, SOC, Board and System; worked with Fortune 100 global companies like Sun and Cisco. He was co-founder of two successful startups RMI (acquired by NetLogic) & SiSilk. Qualities that set him apart are his natural instinct to predict execution flaw ahead, passion for built-in quality and leading a global engineering team from concept to on-time shipment that delivers customer satisfaction. Dr. Rahman is an active Stanford University alumni with a strong passion to develop environmentally friendly technologies to build a better world. Mizan has BS EE, MS EE and PhD EE and MS CompSc degrees.

Fred Wu


VP Business Development


Mr. Fred Wu has over 30 years experience in Semiconductor industries business development, executive management and operation. In addition to gaining experience with numerous companies including Philips, Siemens Infineon, Applied Materials and WSMC, which merged by TSMC, he was responsible for various technical positions and product market and business development, Fred was also investment partners of Sierra Tech and Wealthy Linkage to provide financial advises to several financial institutions in global IC design houses investment strategies. Fred is enthusiastic for continuing success including early stage technology development, strategic marketing, technical competitive landscape analysis, as well as the ability to execute operational business plan.

Ion Opris Ph.D.


Technical Advisor


Ion Opris has more than 22 years of experience in the semiconductor industry. Ion has worked for National Semiconductor in the Data Acquisition group, Core Intl., IAMSAT, in Bucharest, Romania and was the VP of product development at Mcube Inc. He has a strong background in analog and digital designs, solid state device physics, RF designs and spread spectrum techniques and MEMS based systems. Opris has more than 50 publications and holds over 60 patents in several circuit design areas. He has 100+ unique works in MEMS and Gyroscope based designs, CCD & CMOS light sensors, DSL, Ethernet, RFID, Wi-Fi, hi-performance A/D converters and A/V interfaces. Opris completed his MS in electrical engineering and PhD from Stanford University and graduation from Bucharest Polytechnic Institute in 1986.

Dr. Syed Kamrul Islam


Advisor


Dr. Islam is leading the research efforts of the Analog VLSI and Devices Research Group at the University of Tennessee. He is also the coordinator of the UT/ORNL Joint Program in Mixed Signal VLSI and Monolithic Sensor. As an affiliated faculty member of The University of Tennessee Center for Environmental Biotechnology, he is leading the efforts to develop Biophotonic Biosensors using genetically engineered whole-cell bioreporters on integrated circuits. His current research interests are: semiconductor devices, high temperature electronics, analog and mixed-signal circuit design, bio-microelectronics, and nanotechnology . Dr. Syed Kamrul Islam received his B.Sc. in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology and M.S. and Ph.D. in Electrical and Systems Engineering from the University of Connecticut.

Dr. Sohel Imtiaz


Advisor


Dr. Sohel Imtiaz currently is the President and CEO of Innovative Power Management Inc. Dr. Imtiaz has 26 years of experience in semiconductor industry and academia. He received his Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from Arizona State University, Tempe, Arizona in May 1997. Before that he got his BS and MS in Electrical Engineering from BUET in 1988 and 1990, respectively. Dr. Imtiaz held various engineering and management positions at leading Silicon Valley companies, such as, Microlinear, Fairchild, Micrel and Linear Dimensions. Dr. Imtiaz has also been involved in a number of start-up companies. He has contributed in various positions, such as VP of Engineering, Director of Engineering, Analog Design Engineering Manager, Design Engineer, Process Design Kit development, and IP development. His areas of interest are Power Management ICs (PMICs/PMUs), SoCs, Filters, DC-DC and AC-DC regulators, LDOs, LED drivers, A-to-D converters, MEMS, RF Transceivers, and Solar cells. Dr. Imtiaz holds six US patents, published four IEEE Transaction papers and presented eight technical papers in IEEE conferences.
